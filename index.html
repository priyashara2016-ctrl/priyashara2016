Welcome!

Welcome to my academic homepage! I am pursuing my Ph.D. in Physics from Banaras Hindu University under the supervision of Prof. Devendra Kumar Mishra. My research focuses on quantum metrology, quantum optics, and nonlinear optical systems. I have collaborated with international groups, including the Quantum Technology Lab at the University of Milan, and presented my work at national and international conferences.

My work is centered on quantum metrology, quantum LiDAR, and nonlinear quantum optics. I explore the use of multi-photonic and nonclassical states for enhancing sensitivity in measurement systems. My research involves theoretical modeling, analytical calculations, and numerical simulations using Mathematica, MATLAB, and Quantum Toolbox in Python.
